2009 - 2013 ID System

ID System are specialists on access control solution. The company develops it own turnstiles, gates, coin machines and software

In ID System I used to work as a project engineer for access control. I was often in contact with customers and finding ideal hardware and software solution to their problems. I was also responsible for upgrades and updates on existing products (turnstiles, gates), software and databases.

2005 - 2008 Advantech Czech

Along with study towards my second degree I took a job for Advantech and used my skills in practise.

In Advantech I was a production engineer in electronic laboratory. Testing PCBs, product reports and amendments to test procedures was my daily bread. In Conel I have learned procedure of testing and developing new product, from testing PCBs, through software to final product. I was able to make efficient test algorithm and make amendments to existing test algorithms.

2005 - 2008 Computer Science and Electronic Design degree

After I understand electronics, ones and zeros, I wanted to know how programming and design affects the hardware.

My next steps lead to my second degree with main subjects mathematics, physic, electronic design, Computer Science and Data transmission. This study told me in depth the theory behind the scenes of electronic and electric engineering, Deep understanding of information technology hardware and the fundamentals of programming process.

2002 - 2005 Electronic Engineering degree

I have graduated from electronic engineering and automation in 2005 This education was focused on the practical site of engineering.

Since my youth I grew hungry for understanding how things work and there began my life. With main subjects, Mathematics, Physic, Electronic engineering, Automation, Electronic and Electric machines and tools. In this school I gathered deep understanding about hardware of electronic and electric. I have learned how to work with all sort of equipment and how to work effective in laboratory environment.
